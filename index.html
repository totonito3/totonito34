Queue Simulation in Java, Spring 2017
Developed a program to illustrate the concept of queues in data structure. In the program, it is assumed that each number in the queue represent a person in the line waiting to be served at a restaurant. Here, the concept of First In First Out (FIFO) is being illustrated. This program could be used anywhere from a restaurant to the DMV waiting line system.
